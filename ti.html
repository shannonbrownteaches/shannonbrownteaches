Technology Integration

From coding pathways for robots to augmenting reality to explore planets in real time and space, technology enhances our learning experiences in the classroom every day. In my experience as an educator, implementing technology has helped me:


  1. Facilitate collaboration and communication
  2. Strengthen student engagement and allow students to become active learners
  3. Provide multiple accommodations for struggling students, challenge gifted students and essentially individualize instruction for all
  4. Provide language support for language learners
  5. Save time so I can work 1 on 1 with more students

In my classroom, I pair technology with instruction daily to allow students the opportunity to create, enhance and explore various topics. Some of our class favorites of integrating technology include:


  1. Utilizing the Night Sky App to augment reality to help students identify stars, planets, constellations and satellites in real time
  2. Coding and designing a pathway with Dash and Dot so robots can safely follow a path of sequenced steps to avoid bumping into hazardous objects
  3. Differentiating math and reading skills with XTRA math and reading A-Z to practice building fluency
  4. Collaborating and Communicating information through flipgrid, quizlet live, kahoot, peardeck..etc.
  5. Exploring excel by following a code to color in cells to reveal a hidden picture
  6. A google expedition to a safari by augmenting reality for students to compare and contrast animal features, habitats and eating habits

Teaching students how to use technology to learn, research, collaborate, solve problems, and explore the world will better prepare them for their future careers.
